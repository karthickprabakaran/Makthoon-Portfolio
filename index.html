About

My Mission

I want to learn the craft of filmmaking the right way and use it with responsibility. My goal is to tell stories that go beyond entertainment—stories that feel real, spark conversations, and change the way people see the world. Through cinema, I hope to connect with audiences in a meaningful way and create films that truly matter.
